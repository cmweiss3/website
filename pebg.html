Political Economy and Behavior Group (PEBG)

The Political Economy and Behavior Group (PEBG) co-organized by Daniel Rubenson and Chagai Weiss brings together faculty and graduate students from UofT working at the intersection of political behavior and political economy, and hosts local and visiting speakers to present work-in-progress. If you are interested in joining PEBG or presenting research to our group, please reach out to Chagai and Daniel!
